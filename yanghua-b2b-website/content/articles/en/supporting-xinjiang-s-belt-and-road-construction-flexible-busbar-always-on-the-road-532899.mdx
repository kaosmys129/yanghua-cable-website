**Supporting Xinjiang's 'Belt and Road' Construction, Flexible Busbars Always on the Road**

Xinjiang, known as the 'Golden Corridor' open to innovation and connectivity, is located in the center of the Eurasian continent and is an important node of the ancient Silk Road. With the deepening of the 'Belt and Road' initiative and Western Development, the gradual improvement of transportation hubs, Xinjiang's trade exchanges with various countries have become increasingly close, and the role of the 'Golden Corridor' has become more prominent. According to incomplete statistics, as of November 2024, Xinjiang's total foreign trade import and export value exceeded 400 billion yuan, with growth rates ranking first in the country.

![微信图片_20250103142840.png](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/6387151135772742497717749.png)

For the 'Belt and Road' and Western Development, Yanghua Technology has always maintained high attention and full participation, from project implementation to investment and factory construction, flexible busbars have always been at the 'forefront.'

![微信图片_20250103142848.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/6387151138338219721812496.jpg)

On December 23, Xu Feng, Party Group Member and Deputy Mayor of Alashankou City People's Government, led a team to visit Yanghua Technology to investigate flexible busbars. Yanghua Technology Chairman Zhang Haidong explained in detail the advantages and applications of flexible busbars, and both sides conducted in-depth discussions about future cooperation. As of November this year, Xinjiang Free Trade Zone's import and export reached 162.92 billion yuan, accounting for over 40%, with Alashankou Comprehensive Bonded Zone and the other three comprehensive bonded zones showing year-on-year import and export growth exceeding 21%.

![微信图片_20250103142852.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/6387151141742754721944546.jpg)

Alashankou City leadership team expressed that whether for import and export trade or local energy-related infrastructure, power systems are a very important sector, and flexible busbars will definitely shine on the local high-current power transmission and distribution stage.

![微信图片_20250103142855.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/6387151144815512455393585.jpg)

In addition to inviting relevant teams from various regions in Xinjiang to visit the company and exhibition hall for technical exchanges, Yanghua Technology also actively participates in the exchange and development between 'Shenzhen and Xinjiang.' On December 26, the company was invited to participate in the 'Shenzhen-Xinjiang Linkage, Belt and Road New Chapter' investment promotion conference. This conference was hosted by the national-level Wujiaqu Economic and Technological Development Zone, aiming to establish trade ties between Xinjiang's 'Belt and Road' and excellent enterprises in the Bay Area. Wujiaqu has rich mineral resources and solid industrial foundation, such as domestic agricultural machinery manufacturing base, steel production and processing base, and various chemical parks.

![微信图片_20250103142901.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/66d795d3-5085-49da-8da6-47e0a79f991f.jpg)

For high-current power transmission and distribution needs, Wujiaqu needs and hopes to have innovative, cutting-edge, high-quality solutions that can solve power transmission and distribution needs while optimizing power energy structure and driving local related industrial chain development.

![微信图片_20250103142904.png](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20250103/6387151149951344417568988.png)

Whether it's Wujiaqu Economic and Technological Development Zone or Alashankou City, Yanghua Technology actively responds and promotes the practice and application of flexible busbars in Xinjiang and even the western regions. In low-voltage high-current power transmission and distribution scenarios, we firmly believe that flexible busbars will leverage product advantages to contribute to national 'Belt and Road' construction and Xinjiang's energy and industrial development.

Spring winds bring prosperity, setting sail to ride the waves. On the afternoon of March 29, Yanghua Technology held a flexible busbar product promotion event for Dongguan alliance partners in Dongguan, Guangdong.

This event holds special significance for Yanghua Technology, being not only the first time introducing high-current flexible busbars to the market through a product presentation format, but also the first large-scale promotion event for partners since the company's establishment. This event was jointly organized by Yanghua Technology's official Dongguan alliance partner Zhongyede Mechanical & Electrical, with over 150 participants from more than 40 companies related to the power industry in Dongguan area. The event was bustling with activity.

![199225a4-fb69-46ff-9362-f31da19f3fa2_副本.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240401/6384756742401133957229062.jpg)

The Chairman of Yanghua Technology delivered opening remarks at the event, expressing special gratitude to industry leaders, experts, and friends for attending this presentation, as well as thanking the Zhongyede Mechanical & Electrical team for their efforts in organizing this event. He hoped that through this event, everyone could deepen their understanding of high-current flexible busbars, and establish a platform for communication and learning in Dongguan's power industry through this activity.

The person in charge of Yanghua Technology's Dongguan alliance partner Zhongyede Mechanical & Electrical also stated that as the official alliance partner in Dongguan area, they will uphold Yanghua Technology's values of innovation, responsibility, cooperation, and sustainability, provide dedicated customer service, bridge the gap between headquarters and Dongguan market, and serve as the 'vanguard' for headquarters' national market expansion.

![IMG_5413_副本.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240401/6384756753529982063033384.jpg)

Subsequently, Yanghua Technology's technical engineers formally introduced the high-current flexible busbar product to everyone. From the research and development background, development process, product structure, product selection, product classification, product installation, and practical application cases, they provided comprehensive and detailed explanations of the product from multiple aspects. Currently, Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbars have been successfully applied in photovoltaic, energy storage, super-charging stations, and industrial and civil construction projects in Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Foshan, Shanghai, Guizhou, Hunan, and other provinces and cities, not only safeguarding the 'safety and efficiency' lifeline for customers but also achieving cost reduction and efficiency improvement targets.

![IMG_5469_副本.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240401/6384756758784918386278418.jpg)

During the subsequent networking dinner, attendees expressed their approval and recognition of the flexible busbar products. Now is both a golden era and a highly challenging time for the power industry. Especially after the beginning of 2024, policies and government work reports have pointed out development directions for the power industry through multiple 'compass indicators.' Requirements for reform such as 'dual carbon,' 'new three categories,' 'clean energy,' and 'old power upgrades' are challenges but also opportunities for the power industry. On the path of progress, only innovation is fundamental, and only innovation is the inexhaustible driving force for developing the country's new quality productive forces. High-current flexible busbars are this 'key,' not only locking in safety, efficiency, and quality requirements for customers but also opening the door to exploration of a new era in power transmission and distribution.

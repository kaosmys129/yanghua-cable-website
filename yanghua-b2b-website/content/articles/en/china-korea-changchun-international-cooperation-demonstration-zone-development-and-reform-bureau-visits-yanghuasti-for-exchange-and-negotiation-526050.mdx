**China-Korea (Changchun) International Cooperation Demonstration Zone Development and Reform Bureau Team Visits Yanghua Technology for Exchange and Discussion**

![Meeting Image](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240130/6384221246019186211537061.jpg)

On January 11, the leadership team from the Development and Reform Bureau of China-Korea (Changchun) International Cooperation Demonstration Zone visited Yanghua Technology headquarters and production base for exchange and discussion. This exchange focused on Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbars, jointly exploring future cooperation potential and discussing cooperation development models.

**Exploring Cooperation Development**

![Meeting Image 2](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240130/6384221246030171515738122.jpg)

During the exchange meeting, Yanghua Technology's sales department head provided detailed introductions about company conditions, development history, and basic information about high-current flexible busbar products, application scenarios, and demonstration cases. The China-Korea Development and Reform participating leaders first recognized the innovative advantages and unlimited market potential of Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbar products, hoping that future cooperation could explore more cooperation methods and models beyond project cooperation. They also stated that schools, hospitals, and other large projects will gradually move into the park starting in 2025, with huge market development potential for high-current flexible busbar products, and there will be substantial electrical equipment demand for surrounding supporting infrastructure.

![Factory Visit Image](http://yanghuasti.jx5.szbdk.cn/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240130/6384221246025430986522969.jpg)

In the afternoon, the China-Korea Development and Reform Bureau leadership team visited Yanghua Technology's Longgang manufacturing base for observation, gaining detailed understanding of flexible busbar research and development and production processes, visiting flexible busbar production lines, electrical laboratories, physical and chemical laboratories, and physical mechanical laboratories.

They mentioned that Yanghua Technology's flexible busbars are innovative products that break through traditional high-current busbar product limitations. Advanced manufacturing processes can greatly reduce copper consumption, improve copper saving rates, while ensuring stable and efficient current output. Such high-quality, advanced products are exactly the breakthrough point for the demonstration zone to break through traditional thinking. Innovative and stable products can ensure the demonstration zone's high-quality development, from 'inside' to 'outside' toward high-level development.

![China-Korea Zone Image](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240130/6384221284160254762881514.png)

**China-Korea (Changchun) International Cooperation Demonstration Zone**

The China-Korea (Changchun) International Cooperation Demonstration Zone was approved by the State Council on April 21, 2020. It focuses on China-Korea cooperation in industry, technology, trade, and culture, committed to creating a new engine for Jilin Province and even Northeast China's revitalization and development, a leading area for Northeast Asia regional cooperation and development, and a pioneering area for comprehensive China-Korea cooperation across all fields. At the same time, the China-Korea Demonstration Zone is also a demonstration park for national 'energy conservation and emission reduction,' 'dual carbon,' and other sustainable development construction, so all aspects of demonstration zone construction must achieve green and energy-saving goals.

The park's development and reform office proposed to further implement relevant policy organization and flexible busbar market capacity data situations, actively connect with Yanghua Technology, and promote project implementation as soon as possible.

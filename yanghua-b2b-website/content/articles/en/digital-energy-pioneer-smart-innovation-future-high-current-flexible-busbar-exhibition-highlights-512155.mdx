**Digital Energy Pioneer, Smart Innovation Future — High-Current Flexible Busbar Exhibition Highlights**

On September 11, 2024, the 4-day 2024 International Digital Energy Exhibition concluded perfectly at the Shenzhen Convention and Exhibition Center. Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbar exhibition booth was fully upgraded, with multiple flexible busbar solutions and coiled flexible busbars making their debut, attracting numerous domestic and international industry friends to visit!

During the exhibition, leaders from Shenzhen Power Supply Bureau visited the booth for guidance and expressed recognition for the innovation and future development of high-current flexible busbars.

As an innovative product for power transmission and distribution, high-current flexible busbars inject new vitality into the power industry's development toward green and smart electricity.

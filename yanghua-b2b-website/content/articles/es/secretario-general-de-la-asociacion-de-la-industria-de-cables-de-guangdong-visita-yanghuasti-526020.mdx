## English Reference

**Guangdong Wire and Cable Industry Association Secretary-General Visits Yanghua Technology**

On May 14, 2024, Lin Wei, Secretary-General of Guangdong Wire and Cable Industry Association, visited Yanghua Technology for observation and exchange.

![d5a89d9c-9ce2-4912-81dc-c05755d6286f.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240515/6385139228545404182459138.jpg)

Left: Yanghua Technology Chief Technical Expert Du Jinbiao; Right: Guangdong Wire and Cable Industry Association Secretary-General Lin Wei

Under the enthusiastic guidance of Yanghua Technology's Chief Technical Expert Du Jinbiao, Lin Wei visited the exhibition hall at Yanghua Technology's Shenzhen Nanshan headquarters.

![7c1eb965-a2c7-4a11-aa17-b506ceacab93.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240515/6385139232445763397744419.jpg)

During the visit, Du Jinbiao specially introduced the high-current flexible busbar product. The outstanding highlights of flexible busbars include integrated installation design and unique no-joint features. Compared to multiple parallel cables, flexible busbars have high current density and significant copper savings, not only greatly improving safety and reliability but also reducing installation and maintenance costs, bringing revolutionary innovation to the industry.

![9c682443-0c22-43dc-9729-3efb5adb482f.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240515/6385139234124458419346441.jpg)

Lin Wei first affirmed the innovation of flexible busbar products and expressed anticipation for future applications of flexible busbar products, believing that flexible busbars are expected to bring broader market prospects to the wire and cable industry.

![23ff970e-610b-4e08-b324-688073c3078a.jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240515/6385139236043403917316971.jpg)

Guangdong Cable Association, established in 2013, provides services covering wires and cables, material equipment, testing certification, cable accessories, connectors, wire harnesses, and over 2,000 enterprises. The association fully utilizes its advantages of wide information coverage, strong connectivity, and outstanding resource integration capabilities, creating value through service, building industry public service platforms, actively guiding and promoting the forward and positive development of Guangdong's cable industry, playing an active role in promoting industry economic development.

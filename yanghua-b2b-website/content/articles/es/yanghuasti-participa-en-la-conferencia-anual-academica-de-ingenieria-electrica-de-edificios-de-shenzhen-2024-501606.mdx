## English Reference

![6fff7f43-648e-4e89-8ed3-5619f6187d3c(1).jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240429/6384998633849389967800591.jpg)

On April 25, 2024, the 2024 Shenzhen Architectural Electrical Academic Annual Conference hosted by the Architectural Electrical Professional Committee of Shenzhen Civil Engineering and Architecture Society was held at the Hilton Shenzhen Shekou Nanhai. This annual conference was themed 'Chasing Light and Meeting, Bathing in Light and Moving Forward,' with over a hundred participants including experts, scholars, designers, and enterprise representatives from the provincial and municipal architectural electrical fields.

![084f57ad-6ea1-4358-aa4f-240ddc2ad249(1).jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240429/6384998636667701428923398.jpg)

The event was conducted in the format of 'Product Exhibition Display + Academic Presentation Exchange,' with design institute leaders, well-known enterprise representatives, and experts sharing and introducing applications and innovations of electrical industry-related products from multiple aspects including national policies, industry trends, and innovation trends. Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbars also received significant attention at this event.

![122b4020-6f2d-4131-8f63-f59af892b2be(1).jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240429/6384998639642197687072027.jpg)

During the event, Yanghua Technology's Chief Engineer Du Jinbiao comprehensively introduced high-current flexible busbars from aspects including product research and development background, structure, performance, and application cases.

![a59c85d5dfd16078f09409a87779890c(2).jpg](http://www.yanghuasti.com/Data/yanghuasti/upload/image/20240429/6384998648292247585179124.jpg)

At the product exhibition booth, there was a continuous stream of visitors wanting to learn more, all expressing that flexible busbars are an exciting product that the market needs.

Yanghua Technology's high-current flexible busbar is a product researched, developed, and innovated by company experts based on over thirty years of experience in the power transmission and distribution cable industry. It solves numerous problems in traditional solutions for high-current power transmission and distribution products, including high costs, multiple accessories, numerous connections, complex construction, and inability to achieve fully automated production. Currently, high-current flexible busbars have been successfully applied in various project scenarios including super-charging stations, photovoltaics, energy storage, and industrial plants.
